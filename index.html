ABOUT

TAMIYAKI: Manila's Homestyle Takoyaki with a Distinct Filipino Heart

TAMIYAKI was founded in 2024 right in the heart of Metro Manila, bringing the street snack favorite of Osaka into the Filipino kitchen.

Our name, TAMIYAKI, is a portmanteau of the Japanese "Yaki" (to cook) and the familiar Filipino phrase "Tamang-Tama" (Just Right), reflecting our perfect blend of authentic Japanese technique and signature Pinoy flavors.

We pride ourselves on creating a unique, comforting twist on this classic dish, achieving the delicate, non-chewy center that Japanese tradition demands while introducing local notes—like the punch of siling labuyo and the richness of local cheese.
